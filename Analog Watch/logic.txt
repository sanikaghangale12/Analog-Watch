for hour hand
one rotation=12 hr
12 hr->360 deg
1 hr ->36 deg
for h hrs->36h deg
1 min=60 mins
60 min->36 deg
1 min->1/2 deg
for m mins->m/2 deg
thus
for hour hand->(36h+m/2)deg

for min hand
one rotation=60 min
60 min->360 deg
1 min->6 deg
m min->6m deg

for sec hand
one rotation=60 sec
60 sec->360 deg
1 sec->6 deg
s sec->6s deg
